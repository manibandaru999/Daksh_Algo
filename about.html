Who We Are

More Than a Signal Service — We're Your Trading Partner

Daksh Algo Solutions was born out of a simple frustration: watching traders rely on gut-feel decisions and lose capital that better discipline could have saved. We started as a small team of quant traders and software engineers in Hyderabad, determined to bring institutional-grade algorithmic trading to retail investors.

Over the past five years, we've built and deployed dozens of trading bots, designed custom trading platforms for individual traders and small funds, and developed our own signal generation engine backed by machine learning and technical analysis.

Today, our community of 500+ active traders trusts our signals daily — from intraday NIFTY scalps to longer-horizon positional plays on NSE equities. Every signal we send is rule-based, backtested, and risk-managed.
