What We Build

Algorithmic Trading Services

End-to-end automated trading solutions — designed, tested, and deployed for Indian markets by traders who understand the edge that matters.

🤖 Automation

Algo Trading Bots

We design, develop, and deploy fully automated trading bots connected directly to your broker account via API. Our bots handle everything — signal detection, order placement, position management, and exit logic — without any manual intervention required from you.

  • Zerodha (Kite), Upstox & Angel One API integrations
  • Intraday, swing, and options expiry strategies
  • Real-time P&L tracking and alert notifications
  • Configurable risk limits per trade and per day
  • Co-location & low-latency deployment options
📡 Intelligence

Live Trade Signals

Our signal engine continuously scans NSE & BSE for high-probability setups using a combination of technical indicators, volume analysis, and machine learning models. Signals are delivered with clear entry, stop-loss, and target levels so you know exactly what to do when a call comes in.

  • Intraday NIFTY, Bank NIFTY & equity signals
  • Positional calls with multi-week holding periods
  • WhatsApp, Telegram & email delivery
  • 78%+ historical accuracy across 3 years of signals
  • Post-trade analysis report every week
🔬 Research

Strategy Backtesting

Before risking real money, validate your trading idea against historical market data. We provide backtesting as a service — our team runs your strategy across years of tick-level data and provides a detailed outcome report covering returns, drawdowns, Sharpe ratio, and win rate.

  • 5+ years of NSE tick-level data available
  • Out-of-sample forward testing included
  • CAGR, max drawdown, Sharpe & Calmar metrics
  • Walk-forward optimization to avoid curve fitting
  • Delivered as PDF report with scenario analysis
⚡ Derivatives

Options Strategy Builder

Build, test, and automate complex options strategies without writing a single line of code. Our visual strategy builder supports covered calls, cash-secured puts, iron condors, calendar spreads, and custom multi-leg structures — fully integrated with broker APIs for live execution.

  • Visual drag-and-drop strategy construction
  • Real-time Greeks (Delta, Theta, Vega) monitoring
  • Auto-adjust on breach of Greeks thresholds
  • Weekly expiry and monthly expiry optimization
  • Historical P&L simulation before going live
🖥️ Development

Custom Trading Platforms

Every trader's workflow is different. We build bespoke trading dashboards, portfolio trackers, screeners, and execution platforms tailored exactly to how you trade — from single-page apps connecting to your broker to full-stack multi-user SaaS products.

  • Real-time market data feeds (NSE official & third-party)
  • Custom charts, screeners & watchlists
  • Multi-broker account aggregation
  • Mobile-responsive web apps + downloadable executables
  • Ongoing maintenance & feature development
🛡️ Protection

Risk Management Tools

Protecting your capital matters more than any individual trade. Our risk management layer can be added to any existing setup — monitoring your live portfolio, flagging breaches, auto-squaring positions when thresholds are hit, and generating end-of-day risk reports.

  • Real-time portfolio exposure monitoring
  • Maximum daily loss auto-cutoff (kill switch)
  • Position concentration & sector diversification alerts
  • Correlation matrix & beta-weighted delta tracking
  • Daily risk summary reports via email
