FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need coding knowledge?

No. Our bots are configured by our team based on your strategy preference. You just need a broker account.

Which brokers do you support?

We currently support Zerodha (Kite), Upstox, and Angel One via their official APIs.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes — all plans include a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're unhappy, we refund in full, no questions asked.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time and we'll pro-rate the remaining days on your current plan.

Are the signals safe for beginners?

Each signal includes risk levels, position sizing guidance, and stop-loss — making it accessible for beginners with basic market knowledge.

How are signals delivered?

Via WhatsApp, Telegram, and our member dashboard — all updated in real time as market conditions change.
